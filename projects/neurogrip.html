// GOALS

Goals

Model and fabricate a 3D printed hand with individual finger movement, controlled non-invasively via surface EMG, proving feasibility of low-cost neurological prosthetic control.

// METHODS

Methods

SolidWorks design with DFM principles: press-fit filament joints, zero hardware fasteners. Dual tendon nylon wire actuation driven by 5x MG90D servos through a PWM driver.

An Arduino UNO runs a C++ real-time noise-rejection and threshold-based grip classification algorithm on the EMG signal.

// RESULTS

Results

Zero unexpected actuations across 30 validation trials. Dual tendon routing maximizes grip force. Functional prototype from concept in six weeks.
